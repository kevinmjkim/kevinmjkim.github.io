Information About My Dog

My dog's name is Sarang which means love in Korean. He acts like cats and is scared of absolutely everything you could ever imagine. He hates thunderstorms and whines 24/7 until the storm stops. Recently, we forced him to get a sedative because of his restless attitude.

His Identity

He is a mix of Parson Terrier Mix and Jack Russell Terrier Mix. His head the is a pattern of an oreo with black polka dots on his very white fur. He loves his lifestyle of resting just resting. Does nothing from dawn to dusk except sleeping and eating and sometimes using the restroom.
